About this app

A small accessible web app to compute simple interest using the formula:

Simple Interest = (Principal × Rate × Time) / 100
Total Amount = Principal + Simple Interest

Supports time units in years, months and days. When using months or days the app converts them into fractional years (e.g., 6 months = 0.5 years).

How results are rounded: Values shown are rounded to two decimal places for currency display.
Accessibility: Buttons and fields are keyboard accessible and labelled.
